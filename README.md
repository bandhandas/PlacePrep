## 🌟 What is PlacePrep?

> *"The only platform built specifically for Indian placement season — with company-specific problem sets, AI-driven resume scoring, and a streak system that keeps you sharp every single day."*

**PlacePrep** is a full-stack AI interview preparation platform designed to help engineering students and job seekers systematically prepare for technical interviews at top tech companies — from MNCs like TCS, Infosys & Wipro to product giants like Google, Amazon, Meta & Microsoft.

No more scattered resources. No more wasted hours. Just a **focused, gamified, AI-powered grind** from Day 1 to offer letter.

### 🤖 AI Resume Reviewer

> *Your resume, brutally honest — scored by Claude AI.*

Paste your resume and get:

- 📈 **ATS Score** — how well you'll pass automated screening
- 💡 **Impact Analysis** — identify weak bullet points
- 🔑 **Missing Keywords** — what recruiters are actually searching for
- ✅ **Actionable Feedback** — specific improvements, not vague advice

**Built-in tips:**
```
→ Quantify every achievement — "reduced load time by 40%" beats "improved performance"
→ Include ATS keywords from the job description you're targeting
→ Add GitHub and LinkedIn links to prove your work
→ Keep it to 1 page for freshers, 2 max for experienced professionals
→ Use strong action verbs: Built, Designed, Reduced, Deployed, Led
```

Powered by **Claude AI** (Anthropic) for deep NLP analysis of resume content.

## 🧠 The PlacePrep Philosophy

**Most platforms give you 2000+ problems. PlacePrep gives you the right 60.**

We believe in:

1. **🎯 Quality over Quantity** — 60 perfectly chosen DSA problems > 2000 random ones
2. **🏢 Company-specific prep** — Google prep ≠ TCS prep. We tailor both.
3. **🧩 Pattern thinking** — Teach patterns, not solutions. One pattern = 100 problems cracked.
4. **🤖 AI-augmented** — Resume review, interview simulation, and feedback powered by Claude AI
5. **🔥 Consistency** — Streaks, XP, leaderboards — because preparation is a habit, not a sprint
